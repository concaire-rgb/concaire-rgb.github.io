PassQR

PassQR is an innovative personal project that streamlines the process of securely sharing and managing passwords through the convenience of QR codes. By combining cutting-edge encryption techniques with user-friendly design, PassQR makes it easy to generate, store, and access passwords on-the-go, ensuring that your digital life remains both secure and hassle-free. This project not only highlights the importance of cybersecurity but also showcases the potential for simple yet powerful solutions in everyday tech challenges.
